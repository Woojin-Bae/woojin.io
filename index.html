I am Woojin Bae, an undergraduate student at Seoul National University majoring in Chemical Biological Engineering and currently participating in an exchange program at North Carolina State University.
I am deeply passionate about sustainability and advancing eco-friendly technologies, particularly in the field of next-generation lithium metal batteries (LMBs). My research explores sustainable energy solutions through the structural analysis of the SEI layer in LMBs using CryoTEM. Additionally, I have studied quantum dots (QDs) as part of my academic journey, further enriching my understanding of materials science and its applications in energy technologies.
Beyond academics, I actively foster innovation and collaboration through various forums. My leadership in organizing events and securing partnerships showcases my commitment to impactful change and building connections across diverse communities.
