IoTing my room

*some images are generated by AI.

Project Overview

By utilizing Homebridge as a central hub, I have established a fully automated room. The Universal IR controller makes conventional home appliances to work seamlessly. Also, received weather information and automated curtains increase the regularity of my workflow. Finally, a local server hosted by Raspberry Pi, combined with Apple's HomeKit, provided an user-friendly environment.

Technologies Used

Iot integration, Server management, Adobe Premiere Pro
