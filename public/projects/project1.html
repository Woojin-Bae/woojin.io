Consideration on the relationship between microscopic structure and macroscopic shape according to the synthesis conditions of Hollow ZSM-5

Project Overview

This study focuses on observing the microscopic structure and macroscopic morphology changes in ZSM-5 (Zeolite Socony Mobil-5) zeolites with empty internal spaces under various synthesis conditions. Based on the synthesis method of the Zhang group, we varied the crystallization temperature and the Si/Al ratio in the synthesis gel, observing the results using XRD (X-Ray Diffraction), SEM (Scanning Electron Microscopy), and TEM (Transmission Electron Microscopy). While the microscopic structure remained similar under different variables, the macroscopic morphology changed significantly. The change in variables altered the crystal formation mechanism, which is predicted to be due to Ostwald ripening and silicon leaching.

Technologies Used

TEM, SEM, PXRD
