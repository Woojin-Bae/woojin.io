Analysis of LMBs Solid Electrolyte Interphase Components by Cryo-TEM Imaging and Region-Specific Characterization

Project Overview

This research aims to analyze the diffraction patterns of SEI to determine its composition. The study focuses on two main aspects: SEI formation through electrochemical reactions and composition analysis via cryo-TEM imaging. 1. SEI Formation: SEI is formed on a Cu grid using a coin cell setup with specific electrolytes and electrodes. The grid is then washed, dried, and prepared for imaging. 2. Cryo-TEM Imaging and Analysis: The prepared grid is imaged using cryo-TEM. The images are analyzed with Digital Micrograph (DM) to obtain diffraction patterns and other parameters. These are then input into the CONAN program to identify the SEI’s crystalline composition, considering error values and intensity to find the most plausible candidates.

Technologies Used

Cryo-TEM, Digital Micrograph (DM), CONAN
